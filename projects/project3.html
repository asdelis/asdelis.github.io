Six Marketing Focused Data Analytics Cases

These are cases from a marketing class I took that covered basic experimentation concepts. The projects demonstrate practical application of various statistical and experimental design techniques in marketing contexts.

Main Concepts Covered:

  • Potential Outcomes framework
  • Experimentation design and implementation
  • A/B Testing and A/B/n Testing methodologies
  • Group Sequential Designs
  • Propensity Score Matching and Weighting
  • Uplift modeling with Causal Machine Learning
  • Heterogeneous Treatment Effects analysis
  • Fixed Effects modeling
  • Difference-in-Differences approach
  • Synthetic Control methods

Skills Developed: Through these cases, I gained hands-on experience with advanced statistical techniques for causal inference, experimental design, and treatment effect estimation - all crucial skills for data-driven marketing decision making.

Applications: These techniques are directly applicable to real-world marketing scenarios such as campaign effectiveness measurement, customer targeting optimization, and ROI analysis of marketing interventions.
